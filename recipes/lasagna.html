Lasagna recipe

This recipe involves making lasagna. You will be required to make the meat followed by the sauce

Time to prepare is 1hr 30mins

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Mix the two and put it in the oven
