Chicken Schnitzel

Description

This is a recipe describing how to make chicken schnitzel. It invloves coating the chicken breasts in various ingredients and then frying it in oil

ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Coat the chicken breats in egg
  2. Coat the chicken breasts in flour
  3. Finally,coat the chicken breasts in breadcrumbs
