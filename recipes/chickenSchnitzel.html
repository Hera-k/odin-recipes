Chicken Schnitzel

Description

This is a recipe describing how to make chicken schnitzel. It involves coating the chicken breasts in various ingredients and then frying it in oil

Time taken to prepare 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breasts
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 1 cup of Breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup of oil

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs
  2. Coat the chicken breats in egg
  3. Coat the chicken breasts in flour
  4. Finally, coat the chicken breasts in breadcrumbs
  5. Fry the chicken in oil until golden brown
